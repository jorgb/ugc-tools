Since we have a grid of 1920 ticks, and we have two notes starting at offset 0x00, and we have one offset at the end
that is 0x87, we can try 1920 - 0x87 = 1785, since we have 7 entries with 0xff, and 1785/7 = 255 (0xff) it seems that
the offset(s) above 127 with no pad info are there to count the ticks in a 1920 counter for our sequence.

This means every grid we see will start a counter at 0, up to 1920, and we can decipher what notes fall where, and for
entries with a pad entry of 0x80 are fillers.

But, of a pad (A01) starts at 0x2f, what will pad J16 be? By all means there are 160 pads A/J = 10 * 16 = 160.
